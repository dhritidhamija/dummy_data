Title: Incident Escalation Policy

Overview:
This document defines escalation rules for ServiceNow incidents.

Applicable Areas:
- Incident Management
- Network Services
- Access Management

Escalation Rules:
- If multiple users are impacted, escalate to L3 support.
- If there is risk of SLA breach, notify the service owner.
- If the incident is security-related, assign to Security Operations.

Mandatory Fields Before Escalation:
- Incident Category
- Subcategory
- Assignment Group
- Work Notes
