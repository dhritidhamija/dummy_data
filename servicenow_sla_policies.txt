ServiceNow SLA Policy - IT Operations

Overview:
This document outlines SLA policies configured in ServiceNow for IT incidents.

Priority Definitions:
- Priority 1 (Critical): Complete service outage
- Priority 2 (High): Major functionality impacted
- Priority 3 (Medium): Partial impact with workaround
- Priority 4 (Low): Minor issue or request

Resolution Targets:
- P1: 4 hours
- P2: 8 hours
- P3: 3 business days
- P4: 5 business days

VPN Incident SLA Mapping:
- VPN service down for multiple users → P1
- VPN issue for single user → P3
- VPN slowness → P3 or P4

SLA Breach Handling:
- SLA breach must be documented in ServiceNow
- Root cause analysis required
- Preventive actions recorded

SLA Monitoring:
SLAs are tracked automatically using ServiceNow timers and dashboards.
