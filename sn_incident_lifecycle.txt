Title: Incident Lifecycle Management Guidelines

Overview:
This document describes the standard lifecycle of an incident in ServiceNow.

Incident States:
- New
- In Progress
- Awaiting User Information
- Resolved
- Closed

Best Practices:
- Update work notes at every stage.
- Ensure correct categorization and prioritization.
- Obtain user confirmation before resolving incidents.

Common Issues:
- Incidents resolved without proper notes
- Incorrect incident category
- Missing assignment group

Resolution Notes Example:
"Incident analyzed, root cause identified, fix applied, and user confirmed service restoration."

Escalation Guidelines:
- Risk of SLA breach
- Multiple re-openings
- Business critical services affected
